Who I Am

SDS_MEWAN

I'm SDS Mewan, a cybersecurity undergraduate who got into this field the way most of us do — by being too curious about how things break. What started as poking around router settings turned into a genuine focus on network security, penetration testing, and secure system design.

I spend my time between coursework, CTF challenges, and home-lab experiments — setting up vulnerable VMs just to attack them, then writing up what I learned. I care about security that's actually usable, not just theoretically sound.

Outside of security work, I'm building toward a career in offensive security / SOC analysis, and I'm always looking for harder problems to chase.
